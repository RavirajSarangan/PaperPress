How to use this tool

  1. Drop one or more images into the tray.
  2. Reorder them and choose page size and margins.
  3. Click Create PDF — your multi-page PDF downloads instantly.

From camera roll to clean document

Whether it’s assignment pages photographed on your phone, receipts for an expense claim, or artwork for a portfolio, a single PDF is easier to share and print than a folder of images. PaperPress scales each image to fit the page while preserving its aspect ratio and centres it within your chosen margins.

Frequently asked questions

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WebP. WebP images are converted automatically during processing.

Can I control the page size?

Yes — fit each page to its image, or use standard A4 portrait, A4 landscape, or US Letter with selectable margins.
