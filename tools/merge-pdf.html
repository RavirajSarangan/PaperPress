How to use this tool

  1. Drop two or more PDF files into the tray.
  2. Arrange them in the order you want with the ↑ ↓ buttons.
  3. Click Merge PDFs — one combined document downloads.

One document, not seven attachments

Combining a cover letter with a CV, chapters of a report, or scanned receipts into one file makes submissions cleaner and harder to lose. PaperPress copies pages losslessly — no re-rendering, no quality change.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a file or page limit?

Up to 40 files per merge, with no page limit. Since processing is local, the practical limit is your device’s memory.

Will it add a watermark?

Never. The output is your content only.
