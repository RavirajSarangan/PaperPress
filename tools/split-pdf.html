Split PDF — Extract Pages Free, No Upload

To extract pages from a PDF for free: drop the file below, type a page range such as 1-3, 5, 8-10, and choose one combined PDF or separate files per page. Extraction is lossless and happens entirely in your browser.

How to use this tool

  1. Drop your PDF into the tray.
  2. Enter the pages you need — e.g. 1-3, 5, 8-10.
  3. Choose one combined PDF or a ZIP of separate files, then click Extract.

Send only what’s needed

Sharing a 300-page manual when someone needs chapter two wastes everyone’s bandwidth and attention. Range syntax supports any combination of single pages and spans, and duplicates are handled automatically.

Frequently asked questions

Can I get each page as its own PDF?

Yes — choose "Separate PDF per page" and you’ll receive a ZIP with one file per selected page.

Does splitting reduce quality?

No. Pages are copied losslessly from the original.
