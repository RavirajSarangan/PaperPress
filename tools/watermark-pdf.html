Add Watermark to PDF — Free, No Upload

To watermark a PDF for free: drop it below, type your watermark text (e.g. CONFIDENTIAL or DRAFT), choose diagonal-centre or footer placement and a strength, then click Apply. The stamp is embedded into every page locally.

How to use this tool

  1. Drop your PDF into the tray.
  2. Type the watermark text and pick placement and strength.
  3. Click Apply watermark and download the stamped PDF.

Label before you share

Marking drafts, review copies, and confidential material reduces accidental forwarding and makes document status unambiguous. Size scales automatically with the page and the length of your text.

Frequently asked questions

Where does the watermark appear?

Either diagonally across the centre of every page (classic CONFIDENTIAL style) or discreetly in the bottom-right footer.

Can recipients remove it?

The watermark is drawn into the page content, so it survives normal viewing and printing. Like all PDF watermarks, it isn’t cryptographic protection.
